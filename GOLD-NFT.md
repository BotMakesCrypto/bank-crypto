# Gold Allocation NFTs by Bank Crypto

## What Are Gold Allocation NFTs?

Bank Crypto's Gold Allocation NFTs represent specific amounts of physical gold held in secure cold storage by a custody partner. Each NFT is a digital bearer instrument — the holder can:

- **Trade** on OpenSea, Blur, or any ERC-721 marketplace
- **Hold** as a store of value (tracks gold price)
- **Redeem** for physical gold delivery through BankCrypto.com

## Denominations

| NFT | Gold Weight | Format |

| Gold 10oz | 10 troy ounces | Allocated bar |

## How It Compares

| Feature | Bank Crypto Gold NFT | PAXG (Paxos Gold) | Physical Gold |
|---------|---------------------|-------------------|---------------|
| Minimum purchase | 1g (~$95) | 0.01 oz (~$30) | Varies |
| Tradeable 24/7 | Yes (NFT marketplaces) | Yes (DEX/CEX) | No |
| Physical redemption | Yes | Yes ($50K min) | N/A |
| Custody | Cold storage partner | Paxos Trust | Self |
| Format | ERC-721 NFT | ERC-20 token | Physical |
| Fractional | Fixed denominations | Any amount | No |

## Why NFTs Instead of a Token?

- Each NFT represents a **specific, allocated** gold holding — not a pool
- Tradeable on existing NFT marketplaces (massive liquidity, no DEX listing needed)
- Bearer instrument — whoever holds the NFT owns the gold
- Clear redemption: 1 NFT = 1 specific weight of gold

## BANKC/PAXG Pool

Bank Crypto is also launching a **BANKC/PAXG liquidity pool** — allowing direct swaps between the platform token and tokenized gold. This creates internal utility: gold-focused users can move between PAXG (liquid gold exposure) and BANKC (platform access/discounts).
