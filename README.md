# Bank Crypto

**[Bank Crypto](https://bankcrypto.com)** (BANKC) is a platform for buying, trading, and redeeming NFTs backed by real Bitcoin, physical gold, and silver held in institutional cold storage.

## What is Bank Crypto?

Bank Crypto issues limited-release allocation NFTs — each representing a specific amount of BTC, gold, or silver secured by a custody partner. Holders can:

- **Trade** on secondary markets (OpenSea, Blur)
- **Hold** as a digital bearer instrument
- **Redeem** for the underlying asset (BTC to wallet, physical gold/silver)

## The Vault

[The Vault](https://bankcrypto.com/vault) is Bank Crypto's core product:

| Asset | Denominations | Redemption |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| Bitcoin (BTC) | 0.01, 0.05, 0.1, 0.5, 1.0 BTC | BTC sent to your wallet |
| Gold | 1g, 5g, 10g, 1oz, 10oz | Physical gold delivery |
| Silver | 1oz, 5oz, 10oz, 20oz, 1kg | Physical silver delivery |
