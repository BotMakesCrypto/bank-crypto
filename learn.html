What is Bank Crypto (BANKC)?

ERC-20 on Ethereum

Bank Crypto is a platform that bridges physical precious metals and cryptocurrency through NFTs. The BANKC token is the native utility token of the ecosystem.

What Does BANKC Do?

  • Governance: Holders vote on product launches, pool creation, and ecosystem decisions
  • Trading: Freely tradeable on Uniswap V3, SushiSwap V3, and multiple aggregators
  • Priority Access: BANKC holders get early access to limited-edition NFT releases

What are Vault NFTs?

The Vault is BankCrypto.com's flagship product — NFTs that represent ownership of physically allocated gold, silver, or Bitcoin held in secure storage. Each NFT is:

  • Backed by a specific, identified physical asset
  • Redeemable for the underlying asset
  • Tradeable on NFT marketplaces
  • Audited and verified

Who is Behind Bank Crypto?

BankCrypto.com is operated by an ASIC-regulated Australian entity based in Melbourne. ASIC (Australian Securities and Investments Commission) is the Australian government regulator for financial services.
