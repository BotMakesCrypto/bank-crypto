Bank Crypto FAQ

Everything you need to know about Bank Crypto, the BANKC token, and The Vault.

What is Bank Crypto?

Bank Crypto (BankCrypto.com) is an Australian platform that issues NFTs backed by real Bitcoin, physical gold, and silver held in institutional cold storage. The platform is operated by BankCrypto Holdings Pty Ltd, regulated by ASIC.

How do I buy Bank Crypto (BANKC)?

You can buy BANKC token on:

  • Uniswap V3 — swap ETH for BANKC
  • SushiSwap V3 — swap USDC for BANKC

You'll need ETH in a Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Rabby, etc.) to pay for gas and the swap.

What is the BANKC token price?

Is Bank Crypto safe?

The BANKC token contract has been verified as safe by multiple independent security scanners:

  • GoPlus: Not a honeypot, 0% tax, open source, not mintable
  • Honeypot.is: Clean — 0% buy/sell tax
  • De.Fi Scanner: No critical issues
  • Contract is verified on Etherscan, non-proxy, non-upgradeable

What is The Vault?

The Vault is Bank Crypto's flagship product. It offers NFTs that represent real-world assets:

  • BTC Allocation NFTs (0.01 - 1.0 BTC)
  • Gold Allocation NFTs (1g - 10oz)
  • Silver Allocation NFTs (1oz - 1kg)

Each NFT can be held, traded on OpenSea/Blur, or redeemed for the underlying asset.

How does redemption work?

  • BTC: Sent directly to any wallet address you provide
  • Gold/Silver: Physical delivery to your address
  • Basic KYC required for all redemptions
  • No cash redemption — asset-only
